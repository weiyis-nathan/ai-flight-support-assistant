Flight Delay Compensation Policy

Passengers delayed by more than 3 hours may be eligible for compensation, subject to airline review and local regulations.

Passengers delayed by more than 5 hours may request a full refund if they choose not to travel.

Baggage Policy

Each passenger is allowed 1 cabin bag up to 7kg and 1 personal item.

Checked baggage allowance depends on the ticket type. Economy class includes 20kg of checked baggage unless otherwise stated.

Lost Baggage

Passengers must report lost baggage at the airport baggage desk within 24 hours of arrival.

A Property Irregularity Report (PIR) should be filed before leaving the airport when possible.

Refund Policy

Refundable tickets may be cancelled before departure for a full refund.

Non-refundable tickets are generally not eligible for cash refunds, but airport taxes may still be refundable.

Flight Changes

Flight date changes are allowed up to 24 hours before departure, subject to fare rules and change fees.

Special Assistance

Passengers requiring wheelchair or medical assistance should notify the airline at least 48 hours before departure.
